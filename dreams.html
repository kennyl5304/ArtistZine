writer's block is a geniuine condition that deserves to be taken seriously and treated as the problem it is. it is the bane of every artist's existence, particularly for those who feel we are defined by the art we create.

it is in this barren wasteland created by a self-drought of ideas that a single drop of spontaneous creativity can emerge as the savior to our thirst for inspiration.

it is often said that creativity can strikes like lightning. it can hit you when you least expect, and overwhelm you to the point where the idea leaves your head the moment you start to put pen to page.

so embrace the lightning strike and mine it for all it is worth before the ideas fade away into the wastebin of your mind.
